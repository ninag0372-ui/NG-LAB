What is the IPad pro?

The IPad pro is Apple's highest end tablet, which was released back in November 2015. Since then, it's been used by a vairety of people worldwide for various different purposes, from a tool used to take notes by students, to an easily portable tool for digital design and illustrations. This device especially comes into clutch with the purchace of an Apple Pencil, which is commonly purchased along side it, allowing users to semlessly write or draw on the device with ease.

It is currently avalible in two different sizes, the 11 inch and the 13 inch.
